Built on a Foundation of Trust

Denver West Diesel was founded in 2014 by Chris and Stacie Bagley with a simple mission: to build a diesel repair shop that truly values the people turning the wrenches. As a lifelong mechanic, Chris knew firsthand how demanding and skilled the work is. He wanted to create a space where mechanics are respected, supported, and empowered to do their best work.
