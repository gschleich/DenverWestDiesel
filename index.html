Our Story

Denver West Diesel was founded in 2014 by Chris and Stacie Bagley with a simple mission: to build a diesel repair shop that truly values the people turning the wrenches. As a lifelong mechanic, Chris knew firsthand how demanding and skilled the work is. He wanted to create a space where mechanics are respected, supported, and empowered to do their best work.

What started as a vision to work with purpose and pride has grown into a trusted family-owned business rooted in hard work, integrity, and a genuine passion for the diesel industry. From day one we have focused on delivering high-quality diagnostics, repairs, and service the kind we would want for our own vehicles.

Denver West Diesel has set the standard for excellence in diesel engine maintenance and repair. Our highly skilled technicians leverage years of experience and cutting-edge diagnostics to provide a full spectrum of services, from routine fleet care to intricate custom projects. Conveniently located just minutes from downtown, we are committed to delivering superior results and exceptional service that keeps your operations running at peak performance.

At Denver West Diesel, we take pride in building lasting relationships with the people we serve. Whether you're managing a fleet, running your own shop, or keeping your personal diesel pickup in top shape, you can count on us for honest advice, expert service, and dependable results. When you bring your vehicle to us, you’re not just a customer, you're part of our community.

At Denver West Diesel we know what it means to fix it right, not just quickly. With full in-shop transmission and gear rebuild capabilities, custom fabrication, and a team built from within the diesel industry, we provide a level of depth and trust you won't find in mobile-only operations.

Our Services

At Denver West Diesel, we specialize in advanced engine diagnostics and repair using state-of-the-art equipment and technology. Our shop is fully equipped with the latest tools to accurately identify issues and get to the root of the problem quickly and efficiently. Whether it’s a check engine light, performance concern, or something just doesn’t feel right, our experienced technicians have the knowledge and resources to handle it. From routine repairs to complex engine work, we deliver precise solutions that keep your diesel vehicle running strong and dependable.

Denver West Diesel is fully equipped with an in-house transmission and gear shop, offering everything from full rebuilds to new installations. Whether you're dealing with slipping, grinding, or complete failure, our experienced technicians can diagnose the issue and provide a solution that fits your needs. We carry a selection of rebuilt and new transmissions and differentials, and we also offer custom rebuilds for specific applications. Need parts? We've got those too. Whether it's repair, replacement, or parts sales, we’re your one-stop shop for transmission and gear work that gets you back on the road with confidence.

Our team is trained to work with a wide range of vocational systems including utility trucks, dump trucks, construction vehicles, and other specialized diesel-powered equipment. Our team has extensive experience collaborating with leading body manufacturers, giving us in-depth knowledge of various systems and configurations. We can quickly identify faults and perform accurate, efficient repairs. We understand how critical these vehicles are to your business, which is why we focus on minimizing downtime and ensuring every repair is done right the first time. Whether it’s electrical, hydraulic, or drivability issues, we’ve got the experience and equipment to get your vocational fleet back to work.

Let us help you stay proactive so your trucks stay in top condition and your business keeps moving forward.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vehicles do you service?

We specialize in diesel-powered vehicles, including pickup trucks, vocational trucks, RVs, motorhomes, and commercial fleet vehicles. We also work on power equipment and machinery.

Do you offer diagnostics even if another shop can’t figure it out?

Yes. We use state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and advanced software to pinpoint issues that other shops may miss. We frequently help customers who have been chasing a problem without answers.

Can you rebuild or replace my transmission?

Absolutely. We have an in-house transmission and gear shop. We offer rebuilds, new installations, and a selection of ready-to-go units and replacement parts.

What if I can’t find parts for my older RV or truck?

No problem. Our team specializes in hard-to-source and discontinued parts. We can often fabricate or custom-fit components to get you back on the road.

Do you work with fleets?

Yes. We provide customized fleet maintenance programs, diagnostics, and repair services to keep your vehicles running smoothly with minimal downtime.

Do you perform DOT inspections?

Yes, we are certified to perform DOT inspections and help ensure your vehicle meets all compliance and safety requirements.

Do you sell diesel parts?

Yes. We stock and source a variety of OEM and aftermarket diesel parts. Whether you need a replacement or a hard-to-find component, we can help.

Can I schedule preventative maintenance?

Yes. We can set up a maintenance schedule tailored to your vehicle’s usage and needs. This helps prevent breakdowns and keeps your vehicle in top condition.

What is your turnaround time for repairs?

Turnaround time depends on the complexity of the job and part availability. We’re known for fast, efficient service and always keep you updated throughout the process.

Where are you located?

We are conveniently located just minutes from downtown Denver, with easy access for both local and regional customers.

What kind of diagnostic equipment do you use?

We use industry-leading, state-of-the-art diagnostic tools that allow us to read and interpret data across a wide range of diesel systems and manufacturers. This helps us pinpoint issues faster and with greater accuracy.

Can you work with my extended warranty or service contract?

Yes. We’re happy to work with most extended warranty companies. Let us know your coverage details and we’ll help guide the process.

Do you offer towing or roadside assistance?

While we do not offer towing in-house, we can recommend trusted partners in the area and coordinate with them to get your vehicle to our shop as quickly as possible.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can call us directly, use our online contact form, or stop by the shop. We’re flexible and do our best to accommodate urgent needs when possible.

What are your hours of operation?

We are open Monday–Friday, 6 a.m. to 6 p.m. We offer a secure after-hours drop-off, just give us a call ahead of time and we’ll walk you through the process.

Do you work on gasoline vehicles?

We specialize in diesel systems, but depending on the issue and the vehicle, we may be able to help. Give us a call and we’ll let you know what we can do.

How do you handle custom work or modifications?

We’re experienced in custom fabrication, retrofits, and performance-related upgrades. If you have a specific project or need, we’re happy to consult and build a plan that works for you.

Can I drop off my vehicle after hours?

Yes. We offer a secure after-hours drop-off. Just give us a call ahead of time and we’ll walk you through the process.

Are you ASE certified?

Yes. Our technicians are ASE certified and undergo ongoing training to stay current with the latest technology and industry standards.
